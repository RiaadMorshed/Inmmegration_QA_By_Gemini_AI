
IMMIGRATION RULES EXCERPT - SECTION 245(k)

Section 245(k) Overview:
Certain applicants for adjustment of status may be eligible even if they have worked without authorization or fallen out of status. This is known as the Section 245(k) provision.

Eligibility Requirements:
1. The applicant must be an immediate relative of a U.S. citizen (spouse, parent, or unmarried child under 21)
2. The period of unauthorized employment must not exceed 180 days in aggregate
3. The applicant must be otherwise admissible to the United States

Special Notes:
- This provision does NOT apply to employment-based green card applicants
- Time spent as a student working off-campus without authorization counts toward the 180-day limit
- Multiple periods of unauthorized work are counted cumulatively
