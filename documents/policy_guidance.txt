
USCIS POLICY GUIDANCE - Form I-485 Processing

Document Title: Policy Manual - Volume 7, Part B, Chapter 4

Topic: Evidence Requirements for Adjustment of Status

Required Documentation:
1. Birth certificate or equivalent
2. Passport-style photographs (2 copies)
3. Form I-693 (Medical Examination) - must be completed by civil surgeon
4. Police certificates from countries where applicant lived for 6+ months after age 16
5. Evidence of lawful entry (I-94, visa stamps)

Medical Examination Validity:
- Form I-693 is valid for 2 years from the date the civil surgeon signs it
- If USCIS doesn't make a decision within 2 years, a new medical exam may be required
- Vaccination records must be included with the medical examination

Processing Notes:
- Average processing time: 8-12 months (varies by field office)
- Biometrics appointment required for most applicants
- Interview may be waived in certain employment-based cases
